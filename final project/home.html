This websit is to help people learn to emulate games on various consoles.

what is emulation? Emulation is when you use software on a PC to play console games on it. for example you cant get most ps1 or ps2 games onto PC. So you use an emulator which allows you to play those old games on your PC.

The consoles we currently have guides for emulation are: PS1, PS2, PS3 and Xbox. systems like PS4 or PS5 or other newer consoles like Xbox one or series S/X dont really need emulators right now since their games are most likely already on PC.
Nintendo games on the other hand have emulators even for their newest games since they dont allow their games on any other platform other than Nintendo consoles.
